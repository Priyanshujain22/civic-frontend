How It Works

1. Report

Submit your complaint with details, location, and photos.

2. Assign

Authorities review and assign the issue to the concerned officer.

3. Resolve

Track progress and get notified when the issue is resolved.

About the System

Bridging the gap between citizens and administration.

Our goal is to provide a transparent and efficient platform for citizens to report civic issues such as road damage, garbage accumulation, street light failures, and more. Authorities can manage, assign, and resolve these complaints in real-time.

  • Real-time tracking
  • Transparency in administration
  • Direct communication channel
